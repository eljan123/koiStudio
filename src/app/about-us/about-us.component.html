What is Koi Studio?

Koi Studio is a site created by students of WD - 302, for fans of romance anime and manga. While we may be a small team of passionate creators, we strive to bring you the best recommendations, anime, and manga on our favorite genre romance. Our team is made up of anime romance fans, writers, readers, and a web developer who share a deep love for all things romantic in the anime world. If you're interested in contributing, we'd love to hear from you! Feel free to suggest, or if you have your own recommendations to share with fellow romance anime lovers, please contact us.

Koi-Studio Mission

At Koi Studio, our mission is to guide anime and manga fans on a journey through the world of romance. We provide carefully curated recommendations to help you discover your next favorite love story, whether you're a long-time enthusiast or a newcomer. With our passion for romance anime and manga, we aim to simplify the search for the most captivating and heartwarming series, creating an experience that resonates with every fan of the genre.

Koi-Studio Vision

Our vision is to become the leading hub for romance anime and manga enthusiasts worldwide, creating a space where every love story is honored and appreciated. We aim to inspire both new and longtime fans to explore the depth and diversity of romantic storytelling, while nurturing a community built on passion, creativity, and love for the art of anime and manga.
